what is skalo?

skalo is a program to support the inclusion of students with disabilities through a digital solution for teachers that customizes and monitors the reasonable accommodations to guarantee their participation in the classroom.

CUSTOMIZE accommodations according to the student needs.

MONITORS the accommodation's implementation.

motivates teachers and institutions to be inclusive and to practice inclusive practices.

CONSOLIDATED information on early childhood disability.

INTEGRATES the family, the educational institution and the teacher for a coordinated management of the student with disabilities.

why skalo?

Skalo solves the lack of tools that make it easier for teachers to include children with disabilities in the classroom and guarantee their presence, participation and progress.

download skalo

Skalo suggests the most appropriate reasonable accommodations for each student in the classroom

The family creates their child's profile based not only on their disability but on their abilities; areas for improvement and personality.

The teacher performs the initial pedagogical assessment evaluating the main areas of improvement.

Skalo suggests the best reasonable accommodations for the student to participate in curricular activities and achieve their full potential.

Skalo helps monitor the effectiveness of the accommodations to assess their effectiveness.
